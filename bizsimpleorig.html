Why use our switching management service? 

Dedicated Advisor 

We know the importance of people in business. Every time you contact us you will be given a dedicated advisor who is knowledgeable in the business energy market. They'll be your key contact and work with you throughout the switching process. 

You're in Control 

We'll always give you a variety of options, listening to your preferences of suppliers and savings amounts when switching. We notify you before any switch and let you approve or reject our proposal, so you stay in control. 


Transparent Fee Structure

We strike thousands of deals with suppliers every day, so use our  collective power to negotiate better deals than individuals, saving you more.  We'll always tell you what margin we make on any deal, so you can be confident you are getting the best terms for your company.

The Help You Need, When You Need It

Sometimes you need different levels of help depending on what you want to know and do. Our unique service allows you to compare, get advice, and switch via online, email, chat, or phone - whatever works for you, works for us.
